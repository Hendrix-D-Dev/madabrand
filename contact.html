Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process and services

What's your design process? +

Our process includes discovery, strategy, concept development, refinement, and delivery. We work closely with you at every stage to ensure the final result exceeds expectations.

How long does a typical project take? +

Timelines vary based on project scope. Logo design typically takes 2-3 weeks, while complete brand identity projects take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

What do you need from me to get started? +

We'll need information about your business, target audience, competitors, design preferences, and project goals. We guide you through a discovery questionnaire to gather all necessary details.

Do you offer revisions? +

Yes! We include unlimited revisions during the refinement phase to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design.

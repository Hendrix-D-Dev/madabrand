Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process and services

What's your design process? +

Our process includes discovery, strategy, concept development, refinement, and delivery. We work closely with you at every stage to ensure the final result exceeds expectations and communicates your brand's unique story.

How long does a typical project take? +

Timelines vary based on project scope. Logo design typically takes 2-3 weeks, while complete brand identity projects take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation to ensure alignment with your goals.

What do you need from me to get started? +

We'll need information about your business, target audience, competitors, design preferences, and project goals. We guide you through a comprehensive discovery questionnaire to gather all necessary details for creating a brand that truly represents you.

Do you offer revisions? +

Yes! We include unlimited revisions during the refinement phase to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design. Our goal is to create a brand identity that you love and that effectively communicates your message.
